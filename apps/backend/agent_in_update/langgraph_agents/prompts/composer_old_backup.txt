*Why This Place:*
The AI's Places Agent awarded this a remarkable 92/100 match score for multiple converging 
reasons. Category-wise, it's classified as a temple/spiritual site, but it transcends simple 
categorization. This is the Vatican of Goan Hinduism - the principal deity, Mangesh (an

Best For: Rated 95/100 for culture enthusiasts, 88/100 for solo travelers, 85/100 for

- Opening Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM (you're arriving right at opening)
